FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Contact us for more
What services does your web development studio offer?

We specialize in custom website design and development, e-commerce solutions, CMS integration, website maintenance, and optimization for performance and SEO.

How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the project. On average, smaller websites take 4–6 weeks, while more complex websites may take 8–12 weeks or longer.

Do you provide e-commerce website development?

Yes, we build fully functional e-commerce websites with features like product listings, secure payment gateways, and inventory management to help you sell online.

Do you offer website maintenance and support?

Yes, we provide ongoing maintenance packages to ensure your website stays updated, secure, and optimized for performance.

Can I make changes to my website after it’s built?

Yes, we can integrate a user-friendly CMS (like WordPress) so you can easily update content, images, and other elements without needing technical expertise.

What happens if I need help after my website goes live?

We offer post-launch support to address any technical issues, updates, or changes you may need after your site is live.
